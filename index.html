Process

From Data to Cancer Care

A seamless pipeline that transforms raw clinical data into precise, interpretable cancer risk scores.

Step 01
🧬

Data Ingestion

Securely ingest genomic, proteomic, and clinical data via HL7 FHIR-compatible APIs. Works with your existing EHR.

Step 02
⚙️

Multi-Modality Data Fusion

Our proprietary fusion model integrates multi-modal clinical data with population-level epidemiological signals.

Step 03
🤖

AI Risk Scoring

Transformer-based models generate individual risk scores across cancer types, calibrated for diverse populations.

Step 04
📋

Clinical Report

Explainable AI outputs structured for clinician review — SHAP values, confidence intervals, and recommended actions.
